Heart Disease Prediction

Introduction

Heart disease remains one of the leading causes of death worldwide. Early detection and prevention are crucial in combating this pervasive health issue. Our Heart Disease Prediction tool utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to assess an individual's risk of developing heart disease based on various health and lifestyle factors. By analyzing a comprehensive set of inputs, we aim to provide a predictive model that can assist in early intervention and promote better heart health awareness.

Our prediction model considers the following factors:

  • Body Mass Index (BMI)
  • Smoking habits
  • Alcohol consumption
  • History of stroke
  • Physical health
  • Mental health
  • Difficulty walking
  • Sex
  • Age category
  • Race
  • Diabetic status
  • Physical activity level
  • General health condition
  • Sleep duration
  • Asthma
  • Kidney disease
  • Skin cancer

By inputting data for these factors, our model generates a prediction of an individual's likelihood of developing heart disease. This tool is designed to supplement, not replace, professional medical advice and encourages users to consult with healthcare providers for comprehensive evaluations.
